Freiwilliges Soziales Jahr (FSJ)

Voluntary Service

One year voluntary service in social institutions across Germany - perfect for gaining experience.

  • Work in social institutions
  • Monthly pocket money
  • Accommodation often provided
  • Certificate for future career
  • Language course included
